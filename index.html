DROSSIA PALMS HOTEL & NISOS BEACH SUITES

This exclusive boutique hotel emanates a romantic atmosphere enhanced by the magical palm views and the exuberant contemporary design. Located in down town Malia and just a breath away from the central beach it is rightfully considered a jewel amongst boutique hotels in Crete!

Accommodation

Styled to perfection, the beautifully decorated rooms and suites of Drossia Palms Hotel become your accommodation choice, a gateway to a relaxed way of traveling.Soft and bright lines, comfortable beds and inviting mattresses for a memorable sleeping experience and stunning sea views complete the Cretan hospitality experience!

Step into the opulent rooms and the impeccable suites of Nisos Beach and book your accommodation heaven that, combined with the facilities of Drossia Palms Hotel consist the definition of Cretan memorable hospitality.
